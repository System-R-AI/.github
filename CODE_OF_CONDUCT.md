# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

System R AI is committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Focus on technical merit and shared goals
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to hello@systemr.ai. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all System R AI repositories, issues, pull requests, and community spaces.
